Project Presentation Script: Employee Attrition System
Slide 1: Introduction (The Hook)
"Good morning everyone. Today, I am going to present my project: 'Employee Attrition Prediction & Strategic Retention System'.

In today’s corporate world, losing a talented employee is a huge loss for any company—not just in terms of work, but also recruitment costs. My project uses Machine Learning to help HR departments predict which employees are likely to leave and, more importantly, why they are leaving."

Slide 2: Data Exploration & Preprocessing
"For this project, I used the IBM HR Analytics dataset.

First, I performed Data Cleaning, where I removed redundant columns like EmployeeNumber and StandardHours that don't help in prediction.

Then, I used Label Encoding to convert categorical data into numerical format, making it ready for our AI models.

I also analyzed feature correlations, finding that factors like Overtime and Monthly Income play a massive role in an employee's decision to stay or leave."

Slide 3: Model Comparison (The Research Part)
"One of the key highlights of my project is the Comparative Study. Instead of blindly picking one model, I tested three different algorithms:

Logistic Regression: Used as a baseline.

Decision Tree: To see the rule-based flow.

Random Forest: Our final champion model.

As you can see in the results, Random Forest outperformed others with an accuracy of approximately 85-88%. It is an ensemble method, meaning it combines multiple decision trees to reduce error and handle the complexity of HR data much better."

Slide 4: Feature Importance
"My model doesn't just give a 'Yes' or 'No'. It identifies the Drivers of Attrition.

Top Predictors: Monthly Income, Age, and Overtime.

This insight allows HR to move from a 'Reactive' approach to a 'Proactive' approach."

Slide 5: The Streamlit Dashboard (The Live Demo)
"To make this model usable for HR Managers, I developed a Web Application using Streamlit.
In this app, a manager can:

Input Employee Details: Adjust sliders for Age, Salary, Experience, etc.

Risk Diagnostic: The model generates a Real-time Risk Probability Percentage.

Strategic Insights: The app automatically suggests retention tips—for example, if a high risk is due to Overtime, it alerts the manager to redistribute the workload."

Slide 6: Conclusion & Future Scope
"To conclude, this project bridge the gap between Data Science and Human Resources.
In the future, we can enhance this system by:

Adding Sentiment Analysis of employee feedback.

Integrating it with real-time HR software like SAP or Workday.

Thank you! I am now open to any questions."

The "Why" Question: Agar puchein "Why did you use Streamlit?", toh kehna: "Sir, because a Machine Learning model is useless if a non-technical HR manager cannot use it easily. Streamlit makes it accessible."
